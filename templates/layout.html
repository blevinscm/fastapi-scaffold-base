About

Operation Enduring Honor Inc. is a 501(c)(3) located in South Carolina. Our goal is to make it possible for everyone to enjoy the outdoors. We want all families to enjoy picnics, hiking and camping, especially those who have physical challenges. We want to honor our Veterans for their dedication and service to our Great Country and State.
